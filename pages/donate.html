About Our Mission

Lumina Platform was founded in 2018 with a simple yet powerful mission: to ensure everyone, regardless of background or financial status, can experience the transformative power of nature.

We believe that spending time outdoors develops important life skills, builds confidence, and creates a deeper connection with our natural world. Through our fundraising platform, we support initiatives that:

  • Provide wilderness experiences for underserved youth
  • Conserve natural habitats and wildlife
  • Create accessible outdoor recreation opportunities
  • Support environmental education programs
